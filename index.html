OUR COURSES

We offer quality education from Playgroup (PG) to Class 10, based on the CBSE curriculum. Our goal is to build a strong academic foundation along with moral values and creative learning.

PG - KG2 Early Childhood Education

Our playgroup and kindergarten program focuses on motor skills, colors, numbers, basic language, and joyful learning through play and activities.

Class 1 - 5 Primary Education

Primary classes build a strong base in English, Math, EVS, and introduce Hindi, Science, and Computer Skills with activity-based learning.

Class 6 - 8 Middle School

Middle school strengthens subject knowledge in Science, Maths, Social Studies, English, and languages. It also develops analytical and project-based learning skills.

Class 9 - 10 Secondary Education

In Classes 9 and 10, we follow CBSE guidelines with focus on board exam preparation, conceptual clarity, and life skills.
